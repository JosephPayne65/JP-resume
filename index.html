About Me

A different kind of
IT candidate.

My path to IT is not traditional, and honestly I think that is one of the things that makes me a good fit for support work. I spent 14 years as a firefighter and paramedic with the North Aurora Fire Department, where I got really good at staying calm under pressure, communicating clearly, and solving problems fast when things were not going as planned.

A few years back I started pursuing my interest in technology more seriously. I built out a smart home system, set up a home media server, and eventually built and deployed my own web app from scratch. That hands-on curiosity is what led me to go back to school and earn my Associate of Science in Information Technology at Purdue University Global.

I am looking for a role in IT support or help desk where I can keep learning, help people solve real problems, and contribute to a team that values reliability and good communication. I work well with people, I am not intimidated by a challenge, and I genuinely enjoy figuring things out.
